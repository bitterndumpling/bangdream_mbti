# 育美 性格描述

- 中文名：北泽育美
- English Name: Hagumi Kitazawa
- 日本語名：北沢 はぐみ

## 中文
育美没有太多弯弯绕绕的思考习惯，因此一旦认定某件事是好的，就会用最直线的方式全力投入。育美没有太多弯弯绕绕的思考习惯，因此一旦认定某件事是好的，就会用最直线的方式全力投入。她很少让场子冷下来，情绪、热度和行动几乎总是同时到场，因此也特别容易把别人一起卷进自己的节奏。越是和人长期相处，这种底色越藏不住。只是凭着感觉往前冲的时候，偶尔也会显得有些冒进。

## English
Hagumi doesn't have the habit of thinking in many twists and turns, so once she decides that something is good, she will devote herself to it in the straightest way. Hagumi doesn't have the habit of thinking in many twists and turns, so once she decides that something is good, she will devote herself to it in the straightest way. She rarely lets the scene cool down, and her emotions, heat, and actions are almost always present at the same time, so she is particularly easy to involve others in her own rhythm. The longer you stay with someone for a long time, the harder it is to hide this background. When I just rush forward based on my feelings, I sometimes seem a little aggressive.

## 日本語
