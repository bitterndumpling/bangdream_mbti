# 莉莎 性格描述

- 中文名：今井莉莎
- English Name: Lisa Imai
- 日本語名：今井 リサ

## 中文
莉莎让人觉得时尚、开朗、亲和力很强，同时也是 Roselia 里最会顾及他人心情的人。莉莎的存在让这个高压、高标准的乐队不至于只剩锋利，而多了一层能让人靠近的温度。莉莎和友希那的关系尤其重要，因为她是少数能贴近友希那孤高内核、又不轻易被其压住的人。她不是那种会把关心挂在嘴边的人，却总会在别人快要失衡的时候先一步补上那个空缺。放到亲近关系和团体相处里看，这一面通常会更明显。可她越舍不得让别人失望，越容易把压力都悄悄往自己身上收。

## English
Lisa makes people feel fashionable, cheerful, and very approachable. She is also the person in Roselia who is most considerate of others' moods. The existence of Lisa makes this high-pressure and high-standard band not just sharp, but has an extra layer of warmth that allows people to get closer. The relationship between Lisa and Yukina is particularly important, because she is one of the few people who can get close to Yukina's aloof core without being easily suppressed by it. She is not the kind of person who talks about caring, but she always fills the gap when others are about to lose balance. This aspect is usually more obvious when looking at close relationships and group interactions. But the more reluctant she is to let others down, the easier it is for her to quietly put the pressure on herself.
